Before you start — set up Health Auto Export
Takes about 5 minutes. Click to expand the guide.
▶
  • 1
    Install Health Auto Export on your iPhone.
    Search for "Health Auto Export" in the App Store (by Lybron Sobers). It's free with optional paid tier.
  • 2
    Configure it to export to Google Drive.
    Open the app → Automations → Create New → choose Google Drive as the destination. Sign in with your Google account.
  • 3
    Name your export folders using this exact convention:
    health-exports-YOURNAME  and  workout-exports-YOURNAME
    For example: health-exports-alex and workout-exports-alex. These names must match what you enter in the form below.
  • 4
    Share both folders with the coaching service account.
    In Google Drive → right-click your folder → Share → add this email with Viewer access:
    fitness-coach-reader@fitness-coach-490102.iam.gserviceaccount.com
  • 5
    Set the export to run automatically.
    In Health Auto Export → set the automation schedule to Daily. Make sure "Include last 7 days" is enabled so historical data is always present.
  • 6
    Fill out this form, download your config, and send it to get started.
    Once you've generated your config below, email it to kurborg@gmail.com and your daily coaching emails will begin the next morning.
