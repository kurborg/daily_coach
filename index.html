How it works

Fully automated once set up — no app to open, no dashboard to check.

📱
Apple Health
Health Auto Export on your iPhone automatically uploads your daily data to Google Drive.
☁️
Google Drive
Your health export files sit in your own Drive. The coach reads them with view-only access.
🤖
Claude AI
Claude analyzes your metrics against your goals, history, and coaching focus every single day.
📬
Your inbox
